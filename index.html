BIOGRAPHY

Taylor Swift was born on December 13, 1989 in Reading, Pennsylvania, USA. She began her music career at the age of 14, signing with Big Machine Records. Since then, she has released several successful albums in the country and pop genres, winning multiple Grammy Awards. Swift has also dabbled in acting and is known for her autobiographical lyrics and influence on popular culture.
